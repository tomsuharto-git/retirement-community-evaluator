## Description

Part of the nation's largest senior living provider, Brookdale Chatfield offers independent living, assisted living, and memory care on a single campus nestled on over 10 acres of beautifully landscaped grounds in West Hartford. Named a U.S. News "Best Senior Living Community" for memory care, the community features chef-prepared restaurant-style dining, luxury amenities including a sports bar and theater, and personalized care plans that adjust as needs change. Conveniently located near West Farms Mall, restaurants, golf courses and Wolcott Park.

## Financial Plans & Pricing

### Payment Model: Fee-for-Service (Monthly)
Brookdale Chatfield operates on a **flexible monthly payment basis** (not CCRC, no entrance fee required):

- **Custom care plans**: Pay only for services you need
- **Adjustable costs**: As needs increase or decrease, plan and cost adjust
- **Single monthly payment**: Consolidates multiple expenses
- **One-time community fee**: $550 - $6,100 (varies by unit/care level)

### Pricing by Care Level:
- **Independent Living**: Starting at $4,095/month
- **Assisted Living**: Starting at $6,170/month
- **Memory Care**: Starting at $8,025/month

**Note**: Semi-private rooms available at lower costs. Dining and housekeeping included in base rate.

### What's Included:
- Housekeeping and laundry services
- Chef-prepared meals (restaurant-style)
- Educational workshops and classes
- Social activities (happy hours, ice cream socials, excursions)
- 24/7 staff availability
- Emergency alert system
- Transportation assistance

### Additional Costs:
- Care services beyond base plan (medication management, bathing assistance, etc.)
- Personal items through Personal Solutions program
- Guest suite rentals
- Some specialized services

### Financial Assistance:
- Medicaid accepted (may assist with assisted living and memory care through state waivers)
- Medicare does not cover residential care (may cover therapy/medical services)
- Veterans benefits accepted
- Long-term care insurance accepted

### Monthly Cost Range:
**Min**: $4,095 (Independent Living)
**Max**: $8,025 (Memory Care)
**Average**: ~$6,350
