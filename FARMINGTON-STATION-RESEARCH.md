## DESCRIPTION

Farmington Station is a premier assisted living and memory care community featuring 89 assisted living apartments and 45 memory support units, proudly recognized as one of the first in the nation to receive Purple Flag for Dementia Care™ Accreditation. A U.S. News Best Senior Living community for both assisted living and memory care, Farmington Station has earned A Place for Mom awards for "Best Meals and Dining" and "Most Friendly in Senior Living" with a 9.2 review score from 75 reviews. The community features award-winning Brain Healthy Cooking, a state-of-the-art Compass Memory Support Neighborhood®, a movie theater, full-service salon, and flexible respite stays with no minimum stay requirement.

## FINANCIAL PLANS & PRICING

### Payment Model: Monthly Fee-for-Service (No Entrance Fees)

Farmington Station operates on a **monthly rental model** with no buy-in fees or entrance requirements, making it accessible for families seeking quality care without large upfront capital investment.

### Pricing Structure:

**Assisted Living:**
- Starting at: $2,975/month (lowest tier)
- Range: $2,975 - $8,750/month
- Average: $4,346/month
- Some sources cite starting at $3,900/month

**Memory Care (Compass Memory Support Neighborhood®):**
- Included in overall range: $2,975 - $8,750/month
- Specific memory care pricing not separately disclosed
- Typically at higher end of range due to specialized programming

### Price Positioning:
- **Below Area Average:** Starting at $3,900 is lower than Farmington area average of $5,250
- **Competitive Value:** Average of $4,346 significantly undercuts city average of $5,250
- **Cost Variance:** Wide range ($2,975-$8,750) reflects different apartment sizes and care levels

### What's Included in Base Rate:
- Award-winning Brain Healthy Cooking meals (3 daily)
- Assisted living personal care services
- Memory care specialized programming (if in Compass unit)
- Activities and enrichment programming
- Access to all community amenities
- Utilities and maintenance
- 24/7 staffing and emergency response

### Unique Financial Features:
- **No Minimum Stay Policy** - Flexible respite stays available
- **Transparent Pricing** - Multiple sources confirm consistent ranges
- **In-Community Therapy** - FOX Rehabilitation partnership may reduce outside therapy costs
