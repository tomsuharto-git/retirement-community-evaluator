## DESCRIPTION

Brookdale West Hartford is an upscale assisted living and memory care community featuring over 70 private, pet-friendly apartments. A U.S. News Best Senior Living community for memory care, Brookdale was awarded #1 in Customer Satisfaction and has received multiple awards for memory care quality, assisted living activities/enrichment, caregiving excellence, and food/dining services. Part of the Brookdale Senior Living network established in 1978, the community features beautifully landscaped grounds with walking paths, restaurant-style dining, and 24/7 trained staff availability with person-centered care programming.

## FINANCIAL PLANS & PRICING

### Payment Model: Fee-for-Service (Monthly Rental)

Brookdale West Hartford operates on a **fee-for-service model** with no entrance fees or buy-in requirements. Residents pay monthly rent that includes base services, with additional costs for higher care levels.

### Pricing Structure:

**Assisted Living:**
- Starting at: $3,395/month
- Average cost: $4,720/month
- Range: $3,395 - $7,125/month
- Studio apartments start at $4,085/month (private)
- One-bedroom apartments start at $4,240/month
- Shared/semi-private rooms available at lower costs

**Memory Care:**
- Starting at: $6,420/month (private apartments)
- Range varies based on care needs and room type

### What's Included in Base Rate:
- Dining and housekeeping services
- Restaurant-style meals (3 meals daily)
- Emergency alert systems
- 24/7 staff availability
- Daily programming and activities
- Basic utilities
- Building maintenance

### Additional Costs:
- Higher care levels (personalized care plans)
- Personal care products (available through "Personal Solutions" program)
- Pet deposits/fees (community is pet-friendly)
- Additional services beyond base package

### Financial Transparency:
Note: Some reviews mentioned "hidden expenses" and lack of transparency in billing. Prospective residents should request detailed pricing breakdown and clarify all potential costs upfront.

## NOTES FOR SITE VISIT

**Questions to Ask:**
1. Request detailed breakdown of all costs beyond base rent
2. Ask about staff-to-resident ratios in both AL and MC
3. Inquire about staff retention rates and training programs
4. Clarify pet policies (fees, restrictions, size limits)
5. Request sample monthly menu and learn about dietary accommodations
6. Ask about memory care security features and elopement prevention
7. Understand care level assessment process and cost increases
8. Review move-in timeline and availability

**What to Observe:**
- Staff interactions with residents (warmth, patience, professionalism)
- Cleanliness and maintenance throughout community
- Activity level during visit - are residents engaged?
- Dining experience if possible to observe mealtime
- Security features in memory care unit
- Condition of outdoor walking paths and gardens
