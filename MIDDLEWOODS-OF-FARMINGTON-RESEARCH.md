## DESCRIPTION

Middlewoods of Farmington is a small, home-like assisted living community with 73 apartments, affiliated with the prestigious University of Connecticut Health Center in Farmington. Owned and operated by United Methodist Homes, a local faith-based non-profit organization, this intimate community offers a relationship-centered approach to care with unique benefits including on-site geriatric physician and psychiatrist appointments, complimentary YMCA membership for residents, and a 9.4/10 rating from A Place for Mom. The community provides independent and assisted living in studio, one-bedroom, and two-bedroom apartments with full kitchens, emphasizing wellness through partnerships with UConn geriatric specialists and YMCA fitness programs.

## FINANCIAL PLANS & PRICING

### Payment Model: Monthly Rental (No Entrance Fees)

Middlewoods of Farmington operates on a **monthly rental model** typical of assisted living, with no buy-in fees or entrance requirements.

### Pricing Structure:

**Monthly Costs (Wide Range Across Sources):**
- **Low estimate:** Starting at $2,280/month (one source)

**Pricing Context:**

**CRITICAL Questions to Ask:**
1. **Obtain phone number and primary contact** (not readily available online)
2. **Request detailed pricing breakdown:**
   - Clarify $2,280 vs. $5,474 variance
   - What accounts for pricing differences?
   - Exactly what's included vs. additional costs?
3. Ask about UConn Health affiliation specifics:
   - How often are geriatric specialists on-site?
   - Appointment booking process

   - Which YMCA location(s)?

   - How many residents actively use it?
5. Tour full kitchen apartments - gauge actual cooking capability
6. Understand assisted living vs. independent living distinction
7. Ask about community size impact - benefits/limitations of 73 apartments
8. Learn about United Methodist Homes and faith-based mission impact
9. Understand admission criteria and care level assessment
10. Ask about memory care capabilities (or lack thereof)
11. Review typical resident profile and community culture
12. Request sample weekly activities calendar

- Small community feel - intimate vs. institutional?
