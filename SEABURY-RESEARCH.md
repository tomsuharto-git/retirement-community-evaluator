## Description

Founded in 1992 on a former dairy farm, Seabury is a CARF-accredited Active Life Plan Community (CCRC) situated on 66 acres of beautiful Connecticut countryside. Winner of Hartford Magazine's "Best of Hartford" award from 2009-2023, Seabury emphasizes a "Wellness for Life" philosophy with comprehensive care, active lifestyle programming, and the security of future long-term care at no additional cost through their Life Care contract.

## Financial Plans & Pricing

### Life Care Contract (Primary Option)
Seabury offers a Life Care plan where residents are guaranteed living accommodations and appropriate care at no additional cost, even if their needs increase. This includes:
- Entrance fee (amount varies, contact for details)
- Monthly fees ranging from $4,200 - $8,800
- Transition to assisted living, memory support, and nursing care for same monthly fee
- Financial protection through Seabury Charitable Foundation

### Seabury At Home (Off-Campus Option)
An in-home Life Care model for people who prefer to age in their own homes:
- Membership fee similar to on-campus entrance fee
- Access to services and healthcare without relocating

### Monthly Cost Range
**Estimated**: $4,200 - $8,800/month
**Average**: $7,720/month
**Range (all care levels)**: $1,966 - $13,475/month

**Note**: Wide range reflects different housing types and care levels. Independent living starts around $4,200, while skilled nursing can reach $13,475.
