## DESCRIPTION

River Ridge at Avon is a premier assisted living and memory care community operated by Benchmark Senior Living in the heart of Farmington Valley near Routes 202 and 44. Recognized as a U.S. News Best Senior Living community for both assisted living and memory care, River Ridge has earned Benchmark's "Best Meals and Dining" award and provides over 24 years of compassionate care to residents. The community features The Harbor Program for memory-impaired residents, studio/one/two-bedroom apartments, quiet grounds with expansive outdoor courtyard, serene walking paths, library, fitness room, landscaped gardens, wellness centers, private dining, salon, recreation room, bistro, and gazebo for social hours.
