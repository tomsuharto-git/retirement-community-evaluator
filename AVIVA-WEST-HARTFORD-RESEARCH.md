## DESCRIPTION

AVIVA West Hartford is a magnificent historic senior living community situated on the former Vanderbilt estate, originally built by the Sisters of Mercy as a Catholic girls' school in the early 20th century. Registered with the National Register of Historic Places since 1983, this premier community offers independent living, assisted living, memory care, and the innovative "AssistedPlus" program for residents needing additional support. Under new ownership by Lloyd Jones since September 2022 and led by Chris Finlay, AVIVA combines historic elegance with modern senior living excellence in the heart of desirable West Hartford.

## FINANCIAL PLANS & PRICING

### Payment Model: Monthly Rental (Fee-for-Service)

AVIVA West Hartford operates on a **monthly rental model** with no entrance fees or buy-in requirements. This makes it accessible to families who prefer not to invest large upfront capital.

### Pricing Structure:

**Independent Living:**
- Starting at: $2,295/month (some sources)
- Primary range: $3,195/month and up

**Assisted Living:**
- Starting at: $3,495/month
- Range varies based on apartment size and care needs

**AssistedPlus Program:**
- Pricing: Custom (for residents needing more than standard AL but less than memory care)
- Provides tailored cognitive support without full memory care placement

**Memory Care:**
- Starting at: $5,500/month
- Secure specialized programming for dementia/Alzheimer's care

### What's Included in Base Rate:
- 3 meals daily
- Comprehensive housekeeping and laundry services
- 24-hour emergency response system
- Free community-wide Wi-Fi and telephone
- On-site laundry facilities
- Daily planned activities
- Transportation services
- Private kitchenettes in every apartment
- Professional & caring staff 24/7

### Financial Transparency:
Monthly rental model provides flexibility without long-term financial commitment. Costs adjust based on care level transitions.
